Sanrio Characters


Character Information

Cinnamoroll
A white dog who is quite shy but very friendly. He can fly using his ears, and enjoys taking naps on customer's laps.

My Melody
A white rabbit with a pink hood. Shes honest and good natured.

Kuromi
A rabbit or imp-like creature. She's My Melody's rival, and is pretty mischevious.

Hello Kitty
A white kitty, also known as Kitty White. She lives in a London suburb with her family.

Gudetama
An egg that can talk.
